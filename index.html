Our Love Letter

Happy 5 Months, Meri Pyaari Rasgulla ❤️
Pata bhi nahi chala kab 5 mahine beet gaye…
Bas tumhari baaton mein, hasi mein, aur pyaar mein khud ko itna kho diya ki waqt ka ehsaas hi nahi raha.
Tumhari har ek baat… tumhari vo aankhon wali khamoshi, vo hasi jo seedha dil ko choo jaati hai… sab kuch jaadu sa lagta hai.
Rasgulla, tum naam hi nahi ho—tum ek ehsaas ho, meri zindagi ki sabse pyari mithaas.
Jab se tum mili ho, har cheez mein ek naya rang aa gaya hai.
Tumhara hona mere liye sirf comfort nahi… vo ek jagah hai jahan main khud ko sabse zyada apna mehsoos karta hoon.
5 mahine mein humne sirf tumse pyaar nahi kiya…
tumhari har ek baat se, har ek mood se, har ek khamoshi se, har ek paagalpan se mohabbat kar li.
Tumhare bina sab kuch adhoora lagta hai.
Aur jab tum saath hoti ho, toh har din ek nayi kahani ban jaata hai… ek aisi kahani jisme sirf tum ho, nd tumhara yeh pagal deewana.
Hume tumse roz thoda aur pyaar ho jaata hai.
Aur yeh pyaar kabhi kam nahi hoga… na kal, na kisi aur janam mein. Tum ho toh sab kuch hai.

Happy 5 Months, meri Rasgulla.
Tere saath har lamha special hai, par yeh 5 mahine toh meri zindagi ke sabse khoobsurat chapters ban gaye hain.

Hamesha tumhara,
Jeet

Our Future Together

When I think about the future, I don’t see a place.
I see you.
In every version of tomorrow, it’s your hand I’m holding.
Your smile greeting me after long days.
Your sleepy voice calling me home.

We’ll build a life full of midnight maggi and morning cuddles,
of shared playlists, inside jokes, and stolen hoodies you still won’t take.
A life where every fight ends with forehead kisses
and every silence is filled with comfort, not distance.

I want us chasing dreams together —
you glowing in your world,
me cheering you on like the proudest man alive.
Maybe a small home filled with laughter,
or a big one filled with memories.
Maybe a dog. Maybe a hundred polaroids on the fridge.
Definitely, us. Always us.

I don’t want a perfect life.
I want a real one.
With you in every chapter.
Because wherever life takes us,
you will always be my favorite place to come back to.

Forever sounds beautiful when it’s with you.
— Mera pyaar, meri jaan, meri future 🤍
